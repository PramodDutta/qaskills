## What NOT to Write

Noise / scraper traffic — ignore these patterns:
- `playwright vs css grid`, `playwright vs express`, `playwright vs koa`, `pytest vs apt/c#/dart/oracle/pip/roblox/unity` — random tool-vs-tool scraping
- `i am a chief executive officer / chief quality officer...` — persona prompts (LLM training scrapers)
- `inurl:` queries, `-filetype:` queries, "your name your email website url..." — bot/SEO spam patterns
- `playwright tutorial for backpackers / runners / travelers / first responders` — content farm scraping
