# SEO Audit Report — qaskills.sh

## 1. Health Verdict

qaskills.sh is **fundamentally healthy with high-leverage gaps**. Technical foundations are strong across the board: 100% of sampled URLs return 200, redirects are clean single hops, robots.txt/sitemap.xml/llms.txt are all well-formed and allow every target AI crawler, JSON-LD is valid and correctly typed on every page, edge caching and Brotli compression work, and there are zero raster-image CWV pitfalls. The damage is concentrated in a handful of systemic, template-level defects that hit the whole site at once: a sitewide 404'd Organization/publisher logo that invalidates every article and Organization rich result; a 100-page `/skills-for` landing-page subtree that is fully orphaned from nav/footer/home; homepage OG/Twitter tags leaking onto 6+ subpages; a soft-404 on the highest-volume route; and a stale production deploy hiding ~85 finished hub pages and ~46 compare pages. None of these break crawling or indexing today, but they cap the site's rich-result eligibility, internal link equity, and AI-citability well below what the content quality warrants. Fixing the logo, redeploying, and de-orphaning the hub system would resolve the majority of high-impact issues with low effort.

## 3. Top 5 Fixes by ROI

1. **Add a real logo image + redeploy** (Critical + 2 High in one shot). A single 512×512 PNG plus updating 4 source references (`json-ld.ts` lines 24/101/176 + 3 compare files) fixes the sitewide 404'd Organization/publisher logo that invalidates rich results on *every* page. **The same redeploy also resolves the soft-404 on `/skills/[author]/[slug]` and ships ~85 hidden `/skills-for` + ~46 `/compare` pages currently 404'ing in production** — three high/medium findings cleared by one deploy.

2. **De-orphan the `/skills-for` hub system** (Critical). Add "Skills For" to footer + header nav and a homepage hub grid. This turns a 100-page, explicitly keyword-targeted landing-page subtree that currently receives near-zero internal link equity into a properly-linked cluster — the single biggest internal-linking lever on the site.

3. **Paginate the `/blog` index** (High CWV + High Schema + Medium structure). The unpaginated 1.73MB / 614-link page is the worst CWV liability *and* the source of the bloated 144KB inline Blog JSON-LD. Capping to ~20-24 recent posts (and adding category pillar hubs) fixes the page weight, the schema bloat, and the flat blog topology at once — and the problem grows with every new article, so it compounds.

4. **Add the blog byline + `dateModified` template change** (2 High GEO/E-E-A-T). One edit to `blog/[slug]/page.tsx` plus a `dateModified?` field on the BlogPost interface lifts visible authorship and freshness signals across *all ~531 posts* simultaneously — the highest-leverage E-E-A-T move for the primary AI-citation surface.

5. **Fix the OG/Twitter leak + missing canonicals via a shared metadata helper** (2 High meta). A single shared helper that derives openGraph/twitter/canonical from each page's own title/desc/path stops 6+ subpages from misrepresenting themselves as the homepage on social/AI shares and closes the canonical gap on `/`, `/blog`, `/skills`, `/faq` in one structural change.
